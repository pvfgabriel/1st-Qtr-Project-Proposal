PLAY WITH YOUR MIND

Were you able to reminisce your experiences from online classes? Did we have some similar struggles? On this page, we're going to relax our brains! If you're tired from doing your requirements, I hope this helps!

Ready for some mind exercises? Let's do it!

  1. First, be in a comfortable position.
  2. Now, inhale and exhale slowly. After, count until you reach your age.
  3. Then, think of what you would like to do after reading this webpage. It can be anything!
  4. Play some music you would like to listen to as of the moment and enjoy it for a few minutes.
  5. Think of your favorite things, happy moments, and what you did for the past days. Close your eyes for a while.
  6. After, roam your eyes around and try to appreciate your surroundings ^^

Anxiety tips and advice

What do you do when you are stressed? There are times that we get anxious and we worry because of different things. From my personal experience when I feel anxious, I rest (includes things I want to do like watching a drama, eating, or sleeping) first to clear my mind. I believe that when our minds are at ease, it's easier to be productive. I really hope you rest too ^^
